Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Annual dues are collected in January of each year. Quarterly payment plans are available upon request. Please contact the Treasurer for payment options.

Download the Architectural Change Request form from the Documents section, fill it out, and submit it to the board. Requests are reviewed at the monthly board meeting. Allow 30 days for processing.

The pool is open May 1 through September 30, daily from 8 AM to 9 PM. All residents must have a valid pool pass. Guests are limited to 4 per household per visit. Full pool rules are posted at the pool entrance.

Use the contact form below to report maintenance issues in common areas. For emergencies (broken water mains, downed trees blocking roads), call the emergency line at (555) 123-4567.

Yes! Residents can reserve the clubhouse for private events. There is a small cleaning deposit required. Contact the board secretary to check availability and reserve your date.

Board meetings are held on the third Tuesday of every month at 7 PM in the clubhouse meeting room. All residents are welcome to attend. Meeting agendas are posted one week in advance.
